Gallery Of Memories Photography is a provider of Photo documentation services and Video Shooting services that has its main office in the Bandung area. We as a Photo Service Vendor in Bandung, Jakarta, Bekasi, Tangerang, Depok, Bogor, and also reach other areas provide photo services and video shooting services for memory galleries. In this Gallery of Memories we will capture photos that will take you back to a long-passed time. One of our favorite things about photography is our ability to evoke memories. For a moment takes us back in time, to moments that we remember with affection and moments that come with a little nostalgia. That's our "Celebrating the moments that have passed and sharing the touching story behind them".
